Maria Nader

My name is Maria Nader, and I am a passionate Graphic Designer, Illustrator, Videographer, and gamer. My creative journey has allowed me to explore diverse visual mediums—from crafting compelling illustrations and dynamic video content to designing engaging graphics for both print and digital platforms.

My goal is to combine my skills in graphic design, illustration, and videography to deliver innovative and impactful designs—whether on a store shelf or within the pages of a magazine. I am always looking for new opportunities to collaborate, learn, and push the boundaries of visual communication.
