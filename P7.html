Alhoor Saeed

IT Multimedia

My name is Alhoor Saeed, I’m 18 years old and currently in my first year of university, majoring in Information Technology - Multimedia. I’ve always been passionate about creative expression—whether through digital and traditional drawing, video editing, or journaling special memories. I enjoy sketching characters from games and shows I love, like FNAF and The Amazing Digital Circus, and I often use CapCut for fun and personal editing projects. I’m also a huge fan of cozy simulation games like Animal Crossing and Minecraft, as well as horror games like Silent Hill and Resident Evil especially classic video games they are the best - I mostly enjoy watching those rather than playing. I used to create and upload videos on YouTube, which was my first step into the world of digital content. Now, I also enjoy making collage-style aesthetic images that reflect my favorite moments and vibes—both digitally and on paper. Alongside my digital interests, I absolutely love cats and have a growing passion for photography, especially capturing everyday moments and beautiful scenery. My interest in multimedia began in middle school when I started my YouTube channel, which introduced me to digital art, comics, and content creation. Even though I lost access to that channel and stepped away for a while, the pandemic helped me reconnect with those passions. Choosing IT - Multimedia felt natural, and it’s the path I’m taking to achieve my biggest dream—becoming a game developer. Inspired by Arabic gaming YouTubers and their amazing communities, I hope to one day create games that connect and inspire others just like they did for me.
